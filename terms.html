GAIGS Pilot Terms

Version 1.0 · 21 July 2026

Release status

GAIGS is a release candidate until the production operator, infrastructure, payment providers, identity provider and externally audited smart contracts are configured. Screens marked offline, draft, pending or testnet do not represent completed public decisions or transferred money.

People retain authority

JARVIS may explain, navigate and draft. It cannot cast a ballot, approve membership, finalize evidence, publish on a user’s behalf or move funds. Clerks coordinate member-approved workflows and can be recalled under the published constitution.

Financial and blockchain risk

Cryptocurrency and public-fund features can involve loss, irreversible transactions and legal obligations. Users must review wallet confirmations. Mainnet and real settlement must remain disabled before legal review, provider onboarding and independent security audit.

Community and emergency content

Reports are user-generated claims unless marked verified by the visible workflow. Do not use GAIGS instead of official emergency services. Illegal content, fraud and harassment are prohibited.

Applicable law

The platform constitution supplies default digital-process rules only. Applicable national, provincial, municipal, cooperative, election, financial, privacy and consumer law always prevails.
