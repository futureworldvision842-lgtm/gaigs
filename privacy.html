GAIGS Privacy Policy

Effective: 21 July 2026 · Policy version GAIGS-PRIVACY-1.0

Operator identification before public launch: GAIGS is maintained through the futureworldvision842-lgtm/gaigs repository. The Play listing must add the final registered legal entity, postal address and monitored privacy email before production publication.

What GAIGS collects

  • Account data: name, email, phone verification status, city/country and account identifiers.
  • Identity review data: a verification provider may process CNIC and residence evidence. The browser stores only masked/reference data; raw identity documents must remain in restricted provider or protected storage.
  • User content: posts, selected media, messages, company pages, skills, services, proposals, evidence and challenge submissions.
  • Location: device location only after a user action. Public post pins are optional and reduced in precision; exact home coordinates are not public.
  • Transactions: contribution intents, provider receipts, project-ledger entries and user-owned wallet transaction hashes. GAIGS does not request or store a wallet seed phrase.
  • Technical security data: timestamps, audit events, abuse reports and limited device/session information needed for account security.

Why data is used

To create and secure accounts; match nearby communities and services; deliver scoped posts and alerts; run elections and project evidence workflows; provide JARVIS assistance under user-controlled context; prevent abuse; and comply with lawful obligations.

Sharing

Public content is shared at the scope the user selects. Private account, identity and residence evidence is available only to authorized processors or reviewers. Hosting, authentication, identity, messaging, AI and payment providers process data only when configured and must be disclosed in the Play Data safety form. GAIGS does not sell personal data.

Blockchain

Blockchain records are public and difficult to delete. GAIGS therefore keeps CNIC, phone, email, exact location and individual ballot details off-chain. Only wallet eligibility, aggregate audit roots, proposal metadata hashes and transaction receipts may be anchored after an explicit wallet action.

Retention and deletion

Users can request deletion inside Settings or at the web deletion page. Profile and owned personal content enter a purge queue after recent authentication. Narrow records may be retained or pseudonymized only when necessary for fraud prevention, dispute evidence, financial/legal obligations or the integrity of aggregate election results; retained records must not preserve unnecessary profile data.

Children, safety and emergencies

The initial release is not directed to children. Emergency reports are treated as claims until verified; users should contact official emergency services for immediate danger. JARVIS is an assistant and cannot vote, approve, publish or move funds.

User choices

Users choose when to share media, approximate location, public skills, community evidence and wallet access. Android uses the system picker for selected media instead of broad photo-library access.

Changes and contact

Material policy changes will be versioned and announced in the app. Until the final legal operator contact is supplied, privacy requests may be opened through the public repository above; a monitored privacy email must be added before production launch.
